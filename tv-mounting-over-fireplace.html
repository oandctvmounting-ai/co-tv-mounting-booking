TV Mounting Over a Fireplace: Complete Safety and Installation Guide

📅 July 22, 2026 ✍️ C&O TV Mounting Team 📍 Serving Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex
Quick Answer: Yes, you can safely mount a TV over your fireplace -- but you need the right mount (tilt), proper heat clearance, secure stud anchoring, and professional wire concealment. C&O TV Mounting handles all of it across DFW.

Why Over-Fireplace Mounting Is Popular -- And Tricky

The fireplace wall is often the natural focal point of a living room. Mounting there saves space and creates a clean look. But it introduces three real challenges:

  • Heat exposure -- Rising heat can damage TV components over time.
  • Viewing angle -- The screen sits too high for comfortable seated viewing.
  • Structural anchoring -- Fireplace walls (stone, brick, tile) require specialized mounting hardware.

How We Handle Heat

We measure mantle clearance (minimum 12 inches) and use tilt mounts that angle the screen downward. For gas fireplaces, we verify heat output stays within TV manufacturer limits. Heat shields installed when needed.

The Right Mount: Tilt Is Mandatory

Fixed mounts leave the screen too high. Full-motion is overkill unless you need swivel. A tilt mount (-10° to +15°) angles the screen down to seated eye level (42-48 inches from floor center). This is the single most important decision.

Anchoring to Fireplace Walls

Stone, brick, and tile require masonry anchors and proper drill bits. We locate studs behind the veneer or use commercial-grade toggle anchors rated for the TV weight. No drywall-only anchors on fireplace installations.

Wire Concealment Options

  • In-wall: Run cables behind the fireplace chase (cleanest look).
  • Paintable raceway: Blends with wall texture, no wall penetration needed.

Both leave a cord-free appearance. Power cables always get in-wall treatment per code.

TV Sizes That Work Over Fireplaces

55" to 75" are most common. Over 75" may need additional structural support. We verify your wall can handle the weight and that the mount's VESA range matches your TV.

What to Expect on Install Day

  • We arrive with the mount, all hardware, and tools
  • Heat measurement and mantle clearance check
  • Stud/masonry anchor placement verification
  • Mount installation, leveling, and TV hanging
  • Cable routing and concealment
  • Final walkthrough and 24-month warranty paperwork

Common Questions

Can you mount over a wood-burning fireplace? Yes, with proper heat shielding and clearance. Gas fireplaces are easier due to lower sustained heat.

How long does it take? Typically 1.5-2.5 hours depending on wire routing complexity.

Do I need to buy anything? No -- we bring the commercial-grade tilt mount rated for your TV size.
